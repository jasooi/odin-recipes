Andalusian Chickpea Stew

Andalusian Chickpea Stew

Description:

A hearty stew from the Andalusia region in Southern Spain, this dish is a staple in Spanish households. Chorizo bits give the stew its savoury meatiness, while tomato sauce and smoked paprika provide depth and chickpeas bring an element of comfort and heartiness. Perfect for one-pot weekday dinners.

  • 1/2 can tomato sauce
  • 1 can chickpeas, washed and drained
  • 1 chorizo, chopped
  • 1 medium white onion, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • pepper and salt to taste
  1. First, fry the onions in the olive oil for 1 - 2 minutes
  2. Add the smoked paprika and chorizo into the pan and fry for another 3 minutes, stirring well
  3. Add the chickpeas and tomato sauce, and add enough water to cover the ingredients in the pan. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
