Omelette

Omelette

Description:

Omelette is a staple dish in many countries. Lets try making it Asian-style!

  • 2 eggs
  • 1-2 Shallots, chopped finely
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • pepper and salt to taste
  1. Whisk the eggs in a bowl and add pepper and salt
  2. Then, fry the shallots in the oil until fragrant
  3. Turn off the heat when the edges are crispy and centre is just cooked
