Southeast Asian Style Fried Rice

Southeast Asian Style Fried Rice

Description:

Fried rice is the go-to dish for many southeast asian families when no one knows what to cook. As long as your have leftover rice, you can whip up a piping hot, flavourful lunch.

  • Leftover rice
  • 2-3 Shallots, chopped finely
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic, chopped finely
  • Sausage, meat, tofu or protein of choice
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon Chicken seasoning powder
  • pepper and soy sauce to taste
  1. First, fry the shallots and garlic in the oil until fragrant
  2. Add the protein into the pan and fry for another 3 minutes, until cooked
  3. Add the rice into the pan
  4. Add the seasoning powder, pepper and soy sauce and stir well..
